WAVE Radio is a one-person electronic music project broadcasting 24/7 from Toronto, Canada. Underground at heart. Global in reach. Real music, no filler.

1994
It started with a cassette deck
WAVE began in a small bedroom in Toronto. Late nights, mixtapes, and a passion for underground sounds.
1998
First stream, first signal
Dial-up days. 56k connections. WAVE went online and never looked back.
2000s
Building the wave
Upgrades, servers, and a growing global audience. EDM culture evolved—and so did WAVE.
NOW
Still independent. Always evolving.
New tech, same mission. 24/7 EDM, handpicked and broadcast with care.
